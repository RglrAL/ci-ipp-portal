Your International Protection Guide

This portal provides essential information for people seeking international protection in Ireland. Find clear, practical guidance about the application process, living in accommodation centres, and building your life in Ireland.

All information is available in multiple languages and designed to help you navigate your journey with confidence. Whether you're just arriving or have been here for some time, we're here to support you with accurate, up-to-date information about your rights and entitlements. we're here to support you with accurate, up-to-date information about your rights and entitlements.

Living in an accommodation centre

Information about daily life, facilities, services and your rights while living in Direct Provision or other accommodation centres. Find practical guidance on meals, healthcare, education access and community integration.

The application process

Step-by-step guide to applying for international protection in Ireland. Understand the stages, required documents, interviews, decisions and appeals. Learn about your rights and responsibilities throughout the process.

Life in Ireland

Essential information for building your life in Ireland. Access guidance on healthcare, education, employment rights, housing, social services and Irish culture. Connect with support services and community resources.
