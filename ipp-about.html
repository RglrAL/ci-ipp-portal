About Your International Protection Guide



Your International Protection Guide is a comprehensive information portal designed specifically for people seeking international protection in Ireland. Created by the Citizens Information Board in partnership with key stakeholders, this portal provides clear, accurate, and accessible information to help you navigate the protection process and build your life in Ireland.

Our Mission

We are committed to ensuring that everyone seeking international protection in Ireland has access to the information they need, when they need it, in a language they understand.

Provide Clear Information

Deliver accurate, up-to-date information about the international protection process in plain language.

Ensure Accessibility

Make information available in multiple languages and formats to reach everyone who needs it.

Empower People

Help individuals understand their rights, options, and the support available throughout their journey.

Why We Created This Portal

Through extensive consultation with people going through the international protection process, support organizations, and government agencies, we identified a clear need for a centralized, trustworthy source of information. Many people told us they struggled to find reliable information, especially in their own languages, leading to confusion and anxiety during an already challenging time.

This portal brings together essential information from multiple sources into one accessible platform, reducing the stress of searching for answers and helping people make informed decisions about their future.

Who We Are

The Citizens Information Board is the statutory body responsible for supporting the provision of information, advice, and advocacy on social services in Ireland. We have over 40 years of experience helping people access their rights and entitlements.

This portal was developed in collaboration with:

  • International Protection Office (IPO)
  • International Protection Accommodation Services (IPAS)
  • UNHCR Ireland
  • Irish Refugee Council
  • Migrant support organizations
  • People with lived experience of the protection process
